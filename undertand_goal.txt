🚀 Main Features of OpenVisionGuard
🧠 1. Persistent Identity Tracking (Core Feature)

Every person/object gets a unique global ID (e.g., Person_1)

Identity is maintained across frames, occlusions, and reappearances

Works even across different videos or cameras

Re-identification

Similarity search

Cross-session memory

👉 Makes the system smart and searchable

identity

timeline

behavior

alerts

Interactions with others
